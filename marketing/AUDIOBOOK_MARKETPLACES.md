# Audiobook Marketplace Plan

## Positioning

Aura Audio Club is a separate original-audio publishing imprint. It should not
be presented as an API, bot or software product.

## First Products

1. `The Thousand Sweet Recipes Treasury`
   - English and Spanish editions
   - Long-form recipe audiobook
   - Companion PDF index for ingredient lists, timing and recipe lookup

2. `The Thousand Savory Recipes Treasury`
   - English and Spanish editions
   - Long-form recipe audiobook
   - Companion PDF index for ingredient lists, timing and recipe lookup

For large recipe titles, the companion PDF is important because recipe
reference content is difficult to use as audio alone.

## Additional High-Demand Lines

- Spirituality and daily inner peace
- Self-help, confidence, habits and personal renewal
- Angel guidance, guardian angel meditations and symbolic inspiration
- Prosperity, abundance, money calm and money mindset
- Practical income ideas for creators and small digital products

Prosperity and money titles should be written as inspiration plus practical
habits. They must not promise guaranteed income, investment results or magical
financial outcomes.

## Publication Order

1. Finish manuscript and recipe index.
2. Record or generate narration with commercial rights and required AI
   disclosure.
3. Master audio to the target marketplace's technical standards.
4. Prepare cover, description, categories, keywords and sample audio.
5. Publish first on Amazon/KDP and Audible/ACX where eligible.
6. Expand to Google Play Books, Kobo and Apple Books.

## Safety Rules

- Do not sell unpublished or unfinished audio.
- Do not upload copyrighted recipes, celebrity voices, brand-owned stories or
  public-domain material without checking rights.
- Do not promise 1,000 recipes unless the companion index and manuscript
  actually contain them.
- Keep all AI-generation disclosures truthful.

## Marketplace Metadata Template

Title:

```text
The Thousand Sweet Recipes Treasury
```

Subtitle:

```text
1,000+ dessert, pastry, baking and celebration recipes in English and Spanish
```

Description:

```text
A long-form bilingual recipe audiobook designed for listeners who want dessert
ideas, baking structure and a searchable companion recipe index. The collection
covers cakes, cookies, pastries, chocolate, fruit desserts, holiday sweets,
breakfast bakes and simple family-table recipes.
```

Suggested pricing needs to be checked per marketplace after final runtime,
format and royalty option are selected.
